Overview

Text Highlighter is a Chrome extension that provides word-by-word reading highlighting on supported websites. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the extension handles and how.

Data we collect

None. Text Highlighter does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data, browsing history, or website content.

Data stored locally

The extension stores your preferences using Chrome's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.sync). This includes:

  • Your chosen highlight color
  • Your preferred word group size

This data is synced across your Chrome browsers via your Google account (a standard Chrome feature). It is never sent to us or any third party.

Website access

The extension runs only on the following sites:

  • docs.google.com/presentation/*
  • abbaselama.org/*

It reads text content on these pages solely to identify words for highlighting. No page content is stored, transmitted, or logged.

Third-party services

Text Highlighter does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
