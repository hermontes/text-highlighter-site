What it does

Text Highlighter helps you follow along with text word by word. It places a visible highlight box around the current word and lets you step through text at your own pace using keyboard shortcuts.

Supported sites

  • Google Slides — works in both edit and presentation mode
  • AbbaSelama.org — prayer texts with automatic content change detection

Keyboard shortcuts

  • Option+H (Alt+H on Windows) — toggle highlighter on/off
  • . or → — next word
  • , or ← — previous word
  • S — pick mode (click any word to jump there)
  • R — restart from first word
  • G — cycle word group size (1/2/3/5)
  • B — show/hide status badge
  • Escape — cancel or turn off

Settings

Click the extension icon in the toolbar to customize:

  • Highlight color — 10 presets or pick a custom color
  • Word group size — highlight 1, 2, 3, or 5 words at a time

How to use

  1. Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store Coming soon
  2. Go to a supported site (Google Slides or AbbaSelama.org)
  3. Press Option+H (Alt+H on Windows) to start highlighting
  4. Use . or → to move to the next word, , or ← to go back
  5. Click the extension icon in the toolbar to change the highlight color or word group size
